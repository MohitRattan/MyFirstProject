About Us

Rattan's Kitchen is an urban, edgy and industrial New York-inspired eatery. It’s laid-back, casual but buzzing with energy and style. The food is much like New York—a melting pot of cultures! We have wood fired pizzas, a great charcoal steak section, sandwiches, burgers, pastas and bar grub platters. A mega selection of desserts, ice creams, malts and shakes (even spiked ones!), and a super well stocked bar with everything from classic cocktails to beer on tap.
